Primary Vowels is a machine vision and artificial intelligence company founded on a simple premise: the same vowels that form human language also encode the mathematical foundations of modern AI.

Our name is our thesis. A, E, I, O, and U aren't just letters — they represent Artificial Intelligence, Euler's constant, Intersection, the computational complexity that governs scale, and the Union operations that merge data into understanding. Every system we build is grounded in these primitives.

From real-time object detection to multi-modal sensor fusion, we develop vision systems that operate at the intersection of mathematical rigor and production-grade engineering.

Built on first principles.

Every vision system we deliver is measured against the metric that defines our name: Intersection over Union. It is the gold standard for evaluating how precisely a model understands what it sees.

We don't chase benchmarks. We engineer systems where the math holds under real-world conditions: variable lighting, occlusion, sensor noise, and adversarial inputs.

  • 01 Transformer and CNN architectures tuned per problem domain
  • 02 IoU-aware loss functions for tighter bounding and masks
  • 03 Edge deployment with sub-10ms inference latency
  • 04 Continuous evaluation against production ground truth
